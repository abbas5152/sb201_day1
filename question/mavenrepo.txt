Q6.What are the different types of Maven repositories? Discuss.
A6. Total three types of repositories is there
       1)Local
        2)Remote or organisation
       3)Central

1)Local: it will be store in devloper's local pc in m2 folder.
2)Remote: this repo is maintain by organisation only store JAR files ,share plugins etc.
3)Central:It is created by maven itself.in this repo we can get all the plugins,architech etc.
